Kelley & Co. Property Management Services

Tenant Screening

At American Heritage Properties, we place a high priority on finding great tenants for your properties. Our tenant screening process ensures that you rent to tenants who respect your property and fulfill their financial obligations. This service provides peace of mind, as it leads to reliable, on-time rent payments and minimizes property damage.

Property Marketing

We specialize in filling vacancies promptly with high-quality tenants. We employ a comprehensive advertising approach that combines digital and traditional methods to maximize visibility. From online listings to traditional yard signs, we use a diverse range of marketing tools to find the best fit for your property.

Rent Collection

Rent collection at American Heritage Properties is efficient and transparent. Our lease agreements are carefully drafted to clearly outline payment terms, minimizing any potential misunderstandings. We offer tenants convenient payment options, ensuring that rent is collected on time and reducing the administrative burden for property owners.

Maintenance & Repairs

Preserving the value of your property is a fundamental aspect of our services. We manage all maintenance and repair tasks, ensuring that your investment remains in top condition. Our proactive approach not only enhances tenant satisfaction but also protects your investment from costly long-term issues.

Eviction Protection

While we conduct thorough tenant screenings, there is always a small possibility of needing to evict a tenant. In such cases, we provide comprehensive support, including legal guidance and handling the eviction process professionally and empathetically. Our goal is to resolve these situations quickly and minimize any impact on your investment.

Financial Reports

Understanding the financial performance of your investment is essential. American Heritage Properties offers transparent access to detailed financial reports, including balance sheets and income statements, through our online portal. This enables property owners to monitor their investment's performance and make well-informed decisions easily.
